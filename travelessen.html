TRAVEL ESSENTIALS TO TAKE TO JAPAN

  1. Documents & Money

    • Passport (with visa if required)
    • Printed flight & accommodation details
    • Travel insurance info
    • International driving permit (if planning to drive)
    • Credit/debit cards and some Japanese yen (cash is widely used)
    • card (like Suica or Pasmo) for trains and buses

  2. Electronics

    • Smartphone & charger
    • power bank
    • adapter (Japan uses Type A plugs, 100V)
    • Headphones/earbuds
    • Camera (optional, if not using your phone\

  3. Clothing & Accessories

    • Clothing & Accessories
    • Weather-appropriate clothes:
    • Socks (many places require removing shoes indoors)
    • Hat & sunglasses
    • Lightweight backpack/daypack\
